Fandel Lin

M.S. student in CCE at NCKU, Taiwan

Fandel Lin is a M.S. student in Institute of Computer and Communication Engineering at National Cheng Kung University, Taiwan. His research interests lie in Urban Computing, Combinatorial Optimization, and Geographic Information Science. Meanwhile, his researches are recognized by the first place award of Student Research Competition in ACM SIGSPATIAL'18 and the second place award of ACM Student Research Competition Grand Final in 2018-19.

He is currently a member of Urban Science and Computing Lab (UCLAB) and supervised by Prof. Hsun-Ping Hsieh.
